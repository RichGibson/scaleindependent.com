This is a laser cut grating or grill cover. The holes are 1/2". This is a walnut veneer over an MDF core.

![grating](/images/projects/laser_grating.jpg)


Ed then suggested that I make smaller versions suitable for model boats, and he took them to the model boat club where several were snapped up by eager boat builders.

![tiny gratingx](/images/projects/laser_tiny_grating.jpg)


Making architectural trim details for model makers and doll houses would be an easy next step.
