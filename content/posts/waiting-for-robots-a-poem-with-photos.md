I used to sit, or stand, in Vienna and notice things
things that happened, or didn't happen, while I waited
for the Gigapan robot to finish.

Now, mostly, I sit and notice things
and I don't wait
for a robot
to let me know when I am done noticing things.

How do I know when I am done?

Sept 28, 2014, 'later,' probably looking at the Technisches University's 'Future Lab'-a pile of shipping containers made into an art and presentation space.
